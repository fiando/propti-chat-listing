# Competitive Positioning Design: Propti vs OLX, Rumah123, Instagram

## Problem

Propti faces a structural objection from potential sellers at this stage:

> "Kenapa harus bayar di Propti? Di OLX dan Rumah123 gratis, dan pembelinya sudah banyak. Propti kan baru — kalau listing saya tidak ada yang lihat, sama aja dengan listing hantu."

This is a legitimate concern. Any new marketplace faces a chicken-and-egg problem: sellers won't come without buyers, and buyers won't come without listings. The goal of this document is to define a positioning strategy that breaks this cycle and gives Propti a defensible reason to exist alongside — and eventually ahead of — established free platforms.

---

## Reframing the Comparison

The common seller assumption is that all listing platforms are equivalent and the only variables are cost and traffic. That framing is wrong for Propti, and the product strategy must make that visible.

### What "free on OLX" actually costs

| True cost | Detail |
|---|---|
| Time | Manually filling out structured forms for every listing |
| Privacy | Full phone number exposed to anyone, including scrapers and spam callers |
| Lead quality | Open to casual browsers, bots, and competitor agents harvesting leads |
| Signal noise | No way to know who is a serious buyer vs someone just saving for later |
| Listing decay | Listings sink quickly in search results; must be re-posted manually |
| Analytics | Basic or absent — no insight into who viewed or showed intent |

### What "no userbase yet" actually means

A new platform with fewer listings and fewer buyers is not automatically worse for a seller. It can be better because:

- **Less competition per listing** — a property on Propti competes against dozens of results, not thousands
- **Buyer intent is higher** — users who navigate to a focused property tool are searching, not scrolling
- **Contact reveal is tracked** — a single qualified reveal on Propti is worth more than ten unchecked WhatsApp blasts from OLX

The positioning must shift the seller's mental model from "how many total eyeballs" to "how many relevant, trackable, intent-qualified contacts."

## Platform-by-Platform Differentiation

### vs OLX

| Dimension | OLX | Propti |
|---|---|---|
| Listing creation | Manual form, free text | AI parsing from WhatsApp-style text, instant structured output |
| Data quality | Inconsistent — depends on seller effort | Structured, moderated, normalized |
| Phone visibility | Public by default | Gated — requires login, logged, rate-limited |
| Listing competition | Thousands per category, fast decay | Curated volume, moderation filter |
| Seller analytics | Basic views count | Reveal count, WhatsApp click, call click, save count |
| Buyer trust signals | Low — no contact gating, scam common | Moderated listings, authenticated contact flow |
| Pricing model | Free listing, upsell for visibility | Free tier + Premium with included quota and seller tools |

**Propti's core edge over OLX:** Propti is not trying to be the biggest classifieds board. It is the tool that creates a better listing in 30 seconds and protects sellers from low-quality exposure. For an active seller, wasted time on bad leads is a real cost OLX cannot account for.

---

### vs Rumah123

| Dimension | Rumah123 | Propti |
|---|---|---|
| Target audience | Primarily agents and developers | Individual owners and small agents |
| Listing cost | Free tier limited, professional plans expensive | Simple free + Premium at Rp 49k/bulan |
| Listing creation | Long structured form | AI text parser → instant review |
| Speed to publish | 5–15 minutes per listing | Under 2 minutes |
| Phone exposure | Public on most plans | Gated and tracked |
| Minimum commitment | Premium plans often require agent registration | No agent license required |
| Indonesian-first UX | Formal, portal-style | WhatsApp-native, mobile-first |

**Propti's core edge over Rumah123:** Rumah123 is built for professional real estate agents. Individual property owners (homeowners selling privately, small landlords) are underserved and often overwhelmed by the formal portal UX. Propti is built for them: paste your WhatsApp listing text, and you are done.

---

### vs Instagram / Facebook Groups

| Dimension | Instagram / Groups | Propti |
|---|---|---|
| Discovery | Hashtag search, algorithm-dependent | Structured search by location, price, type |
| Listing quality | Photo-based, unstructured text | Normalized fields: price, size, bedrooms, location |
| Contact | DM or public phone in bio/caption | Gated reveal with WhatsApp CTA |
| Analytics | Reach/impressions | Qualified contact intent events |
| Shelf life | Post decays in feed within hours/days | Listing stays active for plan duration |
| Scraping risk | High — open captions with phone numbers | Low — gated contact |
| Searchability | No structured filter | Filter by kamar, harga, lokasi, tipe |

**Propti's core edge over Instagram:** Instagram is a broadcast tool, not a search tool. A buyer looking for a 3-bedroom house in Cibubur does not scroll hashtags — they want to filter and compare. Propti gives buyers exactly that structure. Sellers who list on Instagram are missing buyers who search with intent. Propti captures that demand.

## Positioning Statements

### Primary seller pitch (individual owners)

> "Posting properti semudah chat — paste teks WhatsApp kamu, AI langsung rapikan jadi listing profesional. Nomor kamu aman, dan kamu bisa lihat siapa yang benar-benar minat."

### Secondary pitch (against the "ghost listing" objection)

> "Di OLX kamu bersaing dengan ribuan listing. Di Propti, listing kamu lebih mudah ditemukan pembeli yang sedang aktif mencari — bukan yang kebetulan scroll. Dan karena pembeli harus login untuk lihat nomor kamu, setiap kontak yang masuk adalah kontak yang serius."

### Trust pitch (first-time users)

> "3 listing pertama gratis. Tidak perlu kartu kredit. Tidak perlu daftar sebagai agen."

## Unique Features That Justify Paid Tier

Premium at Rp 49k/bulan must be positioned as returning more value than its cost. The comparison anchor is:

- 1 hour of wasted time answering low-quality leads ≈ cost of 1 month Premium
- 1 missed serious buyer from bad listing visibility ≈ cost of many months Premium

| Premium feature | Why it has real economic value |
|---|---|
| Higher listing quota | Removes the bottleneck for active sellers |
| Unlimited photos | Better listings → higher contact intent → shorter time to sell |
| Featured listing placement | Less competition for attention on a curated platform |
| Contact reveal analytics | Know which listings attract real buyers, cut what doesn't work |
| Priority moderation | Listings go live faster, less idle waiting |
| Richer listing presentation | Creates a better first impression before a buyer reveals contact |

## Counter to the "Ghost Listing" Objection

The objection assumes that traffic today is the only variable. The plan to neutralize it:

1. **Propti listings are Google-indexable** — each listing has a public SEO-optimized URL, so buyers who search Google for "rumah dijual [lokasi]" can find Propti listings without knowing Propti exists
2. **Listings are shareable to WhatsApp** — sellers share their own listing link in property groups, which drives demand without Propti needing an existing audience
3. **Propti amplifies seller's own distribution** — the platform enhances, not replaces, how sellers already share listings
4. **Buyer intent on a focused tool is higher** — fewer tire-kickers, more filter-and-contact sessions
5. **Early sellers get a visibility advantage** — listings on a less-crowded platform rank higher and stay visible longer

The correct framing: Propti is not asking sellers to abandon OLX. It is asking them to create their best listing once, then distribute it everywhere — and track which channel actually converts.

---

## Success Criteria for Positioning

- Sellers no longer frame the comparison as "free vs paid" but as "leads I can track vs leads I cannot"
- First-time sellers complete onboarding and publish a listing without abandoning the form
- Premium conversion rate from sellers who already received one qualified contact reveal
- Sellers actively share their Propti listing URL in WhatsApp groups (organic demand pull)
- Retention rate of Premium sellers after first renewal
